My Tomagotchi

Instructions:

Create your own Tomagotchi! Enter your Tomagotchi's name, click submit and your pet will be born. Make sure you feed, play with and give your pet enough rest. If your Tomagotchi's hunger, sleepiness or boredom reaches 10, your Tomagotchi dies.

  • Hunger: 5
  • Sleepiness: 5
  • Boredom: 5
  • Age: 0
